Our Story

About Us

At Logan'sCafe, we are passionate about delivering the finest coffee experience to our customers. Our skilled baristas craft each cup with care, using only the highest quality beans sourced from around the world. Whether you're looking for a quick espresso or a leisurely latte, our cozy cafe provides the perfect ambiance to relax and enjoy your favourite brew.

From Seed to Cup

Our Process

How every cup earns its name

01

Ethical Sourcing

We partner directly with small farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala — paying above fair-trade prices and building long-term relationships.

02

Artisan Roasting

Beans are roasted weekly in small batches to a precise profile — unlocking every note of chocolate, fruit, and florals the origin has to offer.

03

Expert Brewing

Our baristas are trained to extract with precision — dialling grind, dose, and temp for every single shot so your cup is never ordinary.
